My Adventure Journal - A visit to California

Oct 5, 1848

  • Today I went to california to look for gold at the California Gold Rush & brought my shovel & hat. I learned a new word that described everything that was going on. They say is pronounced "Dank" which means really good.

    • March 12, 1950

  • this time California is growing in popularity. The mood is always up, prosperity rules everywhere, & the standard of living is high . I would say that it is the land of plenty for a new generation of movers & shakers who reinvented the way America would live. Today I coined the term "Post up" which I am told means to stand around without doing much.

    • October 1, 1990

  • Today our new park was created, Yosemite. It is located in the Sierra Nevada mountains. It's famed for its giant, ancient sequoia trees, and for Tunnel View, I also learned a new term when I saw people sitting on the mountains doing nothing. Bum, it means to have or borrow.
